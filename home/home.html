The Rise and Fall of the game platforms

From the 1970s to the 1990s, the video game industry was dominated by the Atari 2600, the Nintendo Entertainment System, and the Sega Master System. The 2000s saw the rise of the Xbox 360, the PlayStation 3, and the Wii. The 2010s saw the rise of the Xbox One, the PlayStation 4, and the Nintendo Switch.

Our project aims to provide a general view for students to realize the trends of the game platforms, which publisher fits for their career purpose,what genres they should do if they want to sell their games for specific region, and whatever infomation they want about game published in our dataset.

  • In Map page: you can see the distribution of the game platforms in the world.
  • In Achievements page :you can see the achievements of the game publishers and their best sales genres in different region.
  • In Workshops page: you can explore the data and whatever the data realtionship you want in our dataset.
